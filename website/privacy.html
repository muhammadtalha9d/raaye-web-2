Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Raaye AI, operated by Heliosoft Ltd., is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use the Raaye web application and related services (collectively, the "Services").

By using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our Services.

Information we collect

Information you provide

When you create an account or use our Services, we may collect information that you provide directly, including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Profile picture (if provided through Google or LinkedIn)
  • Professional goals and onboarding responses
  • Prompts, instructions, ideas, and other content you submit
  • Feedback on AI-generated content

Information from third-party services

You may choose to sign in using Google or LinkedIn. When you do, we receive the information you authorize those services to share with us.

Google. When you sign in with Google, we may receive your name, email address, and profile picture.

LinkedIn. If you connect your LinkedIn account, we may collect information that you authorize LinkedIn to share, including:

  • Name, profile photo, and headline
  • Summary/About section
  • City and country
  • Current and previous work experience
  • Education, certifications, skills, and languages
  • Connection count
  • LinkedIn posts and post performance metrics

We only access information that you have authorized through LinkedIn's OAuth permissions.

Automatically collected information

When you use our Services, we may automatically collect certain technical information, including browser type and version, device information, operating system, IP address, approximate location (such as city or country), and standard server logs. This information helps us operate, secure, troubleshoot, and improve our Services.

Authentication

You can create or access your account using Google Sign-In, LinkedIn Sign-In, or email verification using a one-time password (OTP). We do not collect or store passwords.

Some users may begin using the Service as guest users. Guest accounts allow users to create and save drafts before completing account setup. Certain features, including publishing content, are unavailable until the account is linked to a supported authentication provider. For guest users, we store authentication information necessary to maintain the session, including authentication tokens and an account identifier.

Cookies and local storage

Raaye uses limited browser technologies that are necessary to provide the Service.

Essential cookies

We use a secure, HttpOnly authentication cookie to help keep users signed in when using certain authentication methods. This cookie is required for the Service to function properly and is not used for advertising, analytics, or cross-site tracking.

Browser local storage

We use browser local storage to maintain information required for the operation of the Service, including authentication tokens, account identifiers, onboarding progress, temporary application preferences, and limited user interface preferences. These technologies help provide a secure and consistent experience while using the web application.

How we use your information

  • Create and manage your account
  • Verify your identity and authenticate your account
  • Generate AI-powered LinkedIn content
  • Personalize content based on your profile and preferences
  • Connect with your LinkedIn account when you choose to do so
  • Store your drafts and generated content
  • Improve the quality and reliability of our Services
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraud or misuse
  • Respond to customer support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

AI processing

When you use AI-powered features, your prompts, profile information, sample posts, instructions, and relevant context are securely processed through our backend. To provide our Services, we use the following third-party AI providers:

  • Google Gemini
  • Anthropic Claude
  • OpenAI (used only for limited quality assurance purposes)

Your prompts and AI-generated content are stored securely in our backend to provide the features you use and maintain your content history.

We do not use your personal information or content to train our own AI models. We may use aggregated and de-identified feedback, such as how users interact with generated content, to evaluate and improve the performance of our Services and determine which AI models provide the best results.

Data storage and security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Your data is stored using secure cloud infrastructure, including PostgreSQL databases and Google Cloud Storage for uploaded files.

We protect your information by using:

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for data transmitted between your browser and our Services
  • Encryption at rest for sensitive user data
  • Secure authentication mechanisms
  • Access controls designed to limit access to authorized personnel only

While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, no method of transmitting or storing data electronically is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-party service providers

We work with trusted third-party service providers to operate and improve our Services. These providers only process information as necessary to perform services on our behalf. Our service providers include:

  • Google Firebase Authentication for user authentication
  • LinkedIn for account authentication, profile integration, and publishing content on your behalf when you authorize it
  • Google Gemini, Anthropic Claude, and OpenAI for AI-powered features
  • Google Cloud Platform for cloud hosting and file storage
  • SendGrid for transactional emails
  • Pixabay for stock images
  • Tavily for search and trend information
  • Calendarific for calendar and holiday information

These providers are required to protect your information in accordance with their own privacy and security obligations.

Data sharing and disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With trusted service providers that help us operate our Services
  • With LinkedIn when you choose to connect your account or publish content
  • When required by applicable law, regulation, legal process, or government request
  • To protect the rights, property, security, or safety of Raaye, our users, or others
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of all or part of our business, where permitted by law

Data retention and deletion

We retain your information while your account remains active or for as long as necessary to provide our Services. You may deactivate or permanently delete your account at any time.

If you permanently delete your account, we will remove your account and associated personal information from our active systems, including profile information, generated content, uploaded files, and LinkedIn data associated with your account.

Guest accounts that are never linked to a registered account may be deleted periodically. Some information may be retained where required to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce our agreements, or maintain secure backups for a limited period.
