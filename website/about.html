groupsAbout Raaye

LinkedIn growth shouldn't be a second job.

Raaye is an AI LinkedIn manager for busy professionals. We built it on one belief: the people with the most valuable things to say are the ones with the least time to post them.

Why we built Raaye

LinkedIn rewards two things: showing up consistently, and sounding like a real person. For busy professionals, both are hard. Consistency loses to the next deadline. And the tools meant to help - generic AI writers - produce content so obviously templated that posting it does more harm than good.

We thought the problem was framed wrong. The goal was never "write more posts faster." It was to give people back the hours LinkedIn demands while keeping the quality and voice that make the platform worth being on at all.

So Raaye is not a writer bolted onto a scheduler. It is one connected loop - create, plan, engage, measure - grounded in a persona built from you. The result is a LinkedIn presence that runs on fifteen focused minutes a week and still sounds unmistakably like you.
